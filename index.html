Get to know the game of Football

Football is a game enjoyed by fans all over the country. Here we can see a running back juking a defender on his way to the endzone.

Look at that move!

The game of football includes the following offensive positions:

  • Quarterback
  • Running Back
  • Wide Receiver
  • Tight End
  • Offensive Linemen
  • Fullback

Everyone can enjoy the game of football. Just choose a team and root them on!
